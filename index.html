YOUNG ICON PROFESSIONAL AWARDS 2022

The YIELD Icon award recognizes young professionals & entrepreneurs who has made an impact through their company’s or personal efforts that advanced or made a significant contribution to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and rest of the world. The recipient of this award will have made meaningful impact in his or her field.

Nominations towards the Icon Award is invited on the World entrepreneur day celebrated globally on August 21st upto 21st September 2022. Recipients are profiled, highlighted, and announced prior to the YIELD Professional Awards Gala. Nominees are required to attend nomination day Conference and speak at the Awards Gala.

CRITERIA & REQUIREMENTS

  • Only Indian nationals hailing from Kerala and established at KSA may be nominated for the awards.
  • Minimum of 5 years of work experience contributing to his or her developments
  • Nominee must meet the required points to be considered for the Icon Award.
  • This award is given upon the discretion of the YIELD Icon award selection panel and may not be conferred each year.
  • Incomplete submissions will not be considered.
  • Award categories and titles will be announced post nomination entries closure.

Section 1: Contribution and Legacy - 25 points, 1000 words maximum

  • Describe how has the individual has contributed to the field of interest, as well as how this individual’s contribution through their company has contributed to the advancement of the industry.

Section 2: Professional Experience and Body of Work - 20 points, 500 words maximum

  • Describe the individual’s work through industry and thought leadership. This may include professional association or organization memberships and participation and local or international recognitions

Section 3: Education and Certification -15 points, 500 words maximum

  • Describe how the individual’s contribution through education, academia, and certifications impacted the industry. If applicable, please provide certifications and dates received.

Section 4: Additional Statements - 20 points total, 500 words maximum

  • Mentorship within the industry - 15 points.
    • Describe the individual’s contribution through mentorship within the industry.
  • Awards and Accolades (other awards received) – 5 points
    • Provide a list of awards and dates received.

Section 5: Collateral Material - 20 points total

  • Upload Resume - 5 points.
    • Must highlight years of work experience
    • Resume not to exceed two pages
  • Reference letters – Provide up to three reference letters 15 points
    • Each reference letter can receive a maximum of 5 points each.
    • Examples of reference letters include client/boss, peer, or someone influenced by the nominee
